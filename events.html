Joining a new church is great experience and very exciting. Remember, you’re stepping into a community where love, support, and understanding are at the heart of everything. This new chapter offers a place where you can grow in faith, find peace, and connect with people who genuinely care about one another. Whether you’re seeking spiritual growth, a sense of belonging, or a deeper connection with others, this church is here to walk alongside you. Take your time to settle in, knowing that you are always welcomed with open arms, and that every step you take is part of a beautiful journey.

Welcome to our church family! We are so excited to have you here and are committed to supporting the spiritual growth of everyone willing. Our church believes in the power of young people to make a positive impact, and we offer a variety of programs and opportunities designed to help them thrive in faith and life. Together, let's grow in love, unity, and service as we guide the next generation on their spiritual journey.

Please feel free to stop by and join our congregation. We dedicate every Sunday morning to God. Events on Sunday morning services vary depending on the week of the month. (example: 1st Sunday, 2nd Sunday, 3rd Sunday). Come out and enjoy our Bible Study nights where we serve food and drinks after each service of studying the Lords word. Our Youth Nights are filled with exciting events for our children of the church ages 2-17 are allowed to attend there may be a pajama movie night, a simple pizza night, or a field trip planned to keep our youth involved in the church. Social Gathering or Fellowship is for every member of the church to attend and becoming closer with other members of the church. We like to think of all member as family. Pick a service if not all and come out and praise the Lord with us. We look forward to having you!
